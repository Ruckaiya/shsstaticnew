Welcome to Satbaria High School

SHS has the notoriety of being an upbeat and friendly school. It additionally has a convention of urging its students to make significant levels of progress. Our intake is of blended capacity and we progress in the direction of a shared objective of the improvement of every individual potential. The school is situated in Sujanagar upazila of Pabna and housed in a great structure, which has both indoor and outdoor facilities required for creative, scientific and physical education. Students are the most valuable asset of the country and they need our complete consideration to address the difficulties of the rapid developing world.
